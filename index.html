Not Just an
Ordinary Love Story

We first met at work in 2020 after literally bumping into each other, scattering Camille's papers everywhere. Gelo helped her gather them, and though Camille was too shy to look at him, the moment lingered. On a Thursday afternoon, Gelo found out that Camille's phone was stolen. He offered to walk her to the jeepney stop. This kind act sparked conversations on Facebook, and soon, a simple gesture blossomed into a beautiful love story.
